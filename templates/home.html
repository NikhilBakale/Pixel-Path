How It Works

PixelPath uses advanced steganography techniques to securely hide messages within images, making them invisible to the naked eye.

Secure Encryption

Your messages are encrypted using prime number-based algorithms, ensuring only intended recipients can read them.

Visual Steganography

We hide your data in images by modifying pixels in ways that are imperceptible to humans but retrievable with the right key.

Password Protection

A unique password is generated for each transmission, ensuring only those with the correct key can access your hidden message.
